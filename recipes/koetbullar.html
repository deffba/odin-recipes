Köttbullar

Description

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs lean ground beef
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 3 cups thick cream

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients in bowl.
  2. Leave to swell for 15 minutes.
  3. Roll into ping-pong ball-sized balls.
  4. Fry on all sides on high heat until browned. Lower to low-medium temperature and cook them all the way through
