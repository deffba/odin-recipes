Skogaholmsmacka

Description

Ingredients:

  • 1 piece of Skogaholms bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • (Optional) Cucumber

Steps

  1. Place bread on flat surface or the palm of your hand.
  2. Spread a thin, even layer of butter on the bread.
  3. Add 1-2 slices of cheese.
  4. If it's a special occasion, add 2-4 thin slices of cucumber on top of the cheese.
